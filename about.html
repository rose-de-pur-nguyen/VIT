Hi! I'm Rose

The idea of VIT comes to me with a lot of emotions. Although currently what this platform provides is still rudimentary and ordinary. However, when you read this line, I want to tell you more about my (and soon to be our) missions and goals

VIT stands for Voice Integration Technology

Like eggs, visually they may seem identical to us. Even Leonardo Da Vinci once believed that when his teacher always made him practice drawing eggs day after day. But eventually, Leonardo Da Vinci realized that no two eggs are exactly alike. That story is similar to our voices. Indeed, the voice we possess is truly unique

That's why

I have a passion for innovations about voices. And that's the mission of VIT. VIT aims to bring to society the wonderful intersection of voice, audio, and technology, contributing to making the world more vibrant.

Core Goal

Alongside some sub-projects that will be completed in the near future, VIT aims for a broader goal of enhancing the integration of voice into virtual reality technology to improve user experience.
Imagine being able to create your own space just by using your voice, similar to the generative AI of Photoshop!
